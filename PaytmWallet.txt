If you want to pay using Paytm wallet, here are some easy steps.

Step 1 > Open Paytm app in your android mobile.

Step 2 > Login with your credentials

Step 3 > Add money to your wallet

Step 4 > Click on "Pay" option to pay to merchant

Step 5 > Congrats, you have successfully paid the merchant with e-money.




You can follow the same procedure for Mobiwik Wallet also. 

And also in AmazonPay Wallet
